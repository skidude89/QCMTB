Quebec City Mountain Biking Itinerary

Thursday, September 19 – Vallée Bras-du-Nord - Shannahan

Route: Vallée Bras-du-Nord Route

  • Distance: 29.1 miles
  • Vertical Gain: 3,805 ft
  • Primary Trails: La Boréale Bas, La Grande Légende, Nielsen Est, Nord, and Sud
  • Supply Plan: Gatorade, lunch, candy bars staged at the start of Montée des Boas
  • Gear: Downhill gear/trail gear, swap at supply point

Après: Ti-Oui Snack Bar

Dinner: Bistro St-Malo

Friday, September 20 – Sentiers du Moulin

Route: Sentiers du Moulin Route

  • Distance: 11.3 miles
  • Vertical Gain: 2,764 ft
  • Primary Trails: Super G, Léon, Old Léon, LBC 2, Mammoth, King Kong
  • Supply Plan: Refreshments available at Buvette du Moulin
  • Gear: Can exchange gear between laps; LBC 2 and King Kong deserve DH gear

Après: Nano Cinco

Dinner: L’affaire Est Ketchup

Saturday, September 21 – Empire 47

Route: Empire 47 Route

  • Distance: 15 miles
  • Vertical Gain: 2,665 ft
  • Primary Trails: Belzébrute, Descente du coude, Pad Religion, Mirador, Voisine
  • Supply Plan: Stop by La Souche Microbrewery (10 minutes from the mountain)
  • Gear: Can exchange gear between laps; Descente du coude deserves DH gear

Sunday, September 22 – Sentiers du Moulin - Maelstrom

Route: Sentiers du Moulin - Maelstrom Route

  • Distance: 12.8 miles
  • Vertical Gain: 2,556 ft
  • Primary Trails: Crête du Lynx, Slab City, Granitosaur, Wolverine, Maelstrom
  • Supply Plan: Bring snacks

Lunch/Après: Beer at Buvette du Moulin
